Eco-Friendly Destinations

1. Costa Rica: Known for its rich biodiversity, Costa Rica offers protected rainforests, volcanoes, and stunning beaches.
o Stay in eco-conscious lodges and explore the Osa Peninsula for a firsthand experience of sustainable practices.
2. Galápagos Islands (Ecuador): A haven for unique species found nowhere else on Earth.
o Strict visitor limits preserve the pristine environment.
o Choose accommodations like “The Villa” that prioritize waste reduction and energy conservation.
3. Patagonia (Chile and Argentina): At the southern tip of the world, Patagonia boasts glaciers, mountains, and epic hiking trails.
o Explore Torres del Paine National Park on foot to minimize environmental impact.
4. New Zealand: Surreal landscapes, from fjords to ancient forests.
o Much of the country is part of the national park system.
o Visit Aoraki/Mount Cook National Park and the Wai-O-Tapu Geothermal Reserve.
5. Lake Garda (Italy): While Lake Como attracts celebrities, Lake Garda is an eco-friendly gem.
o Explore the lush shores sustainably and appreciate the natural beauty.
6. Arosa (Switzerland): Arosa prioritizes environmental conservation.
o Enjoy pristine Alpine landscapes and eco-friendly accommodations.
7. Bahamas: The Bahamas’ crystal-clear waters and diverse marine life make it a top eco-destination.
o Support local conservation efforts and choose eco-friendly resorts.
